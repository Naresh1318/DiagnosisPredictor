Diagnosis Predictor

About

Diagnosis Predictor is an application designed to take abnormal lab tests, prescriptions and diagnosis of a patient in the previous year and predict his future diagnosis and also give out the necessary precautionary measures. The inputs given are in the form of a string which should be a sequence of abnormal lab tests, prescriptions and diagnosis. The result is a table which gives the ICD-9(International Classification of diseases) code of the predicted Diagnosis and also the probability of the patient having it. A set of steps to reduce the deleterious effect of the diagnosis is displayed as well.

The input diagnosis must be the ICD 9 code assigned to the patient within the past year. Only the 80 most common chronic diseases can be predicted with an average AUC ROC of about 0.7.

Note : Use d_ for diagnosis ICD 9 codes, l_ for labtests and p_ for prescriptions
